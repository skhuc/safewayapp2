Navigating the "Deals" Page

The main location to view any products with a coupon deal attached is the "Deals" page.

At the top of the "Deals" page, there are various filters to sort the available deals through. The most streamlined view is found under the "Categories" filter.
  1. Select the second filter at the top of the deals page, "Categories."
  2. Scroll down and select desired product category for a drop down list of products with a coupon in that category.
  3. Click the "Clip Deal" button to attach coupon to your account.
View all Clipped coupons under the "Clipped" filter on the Deals page.
Pay special attention to the coupon expiration date, listed above the "Clip deal" button for any selected product; they will vary from listing to listing.
