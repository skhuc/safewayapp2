Member Page

This page lists member specific information, with hyperlinks to other aspects of the app.

If signed in, the user's name is displayed at the top of the page. Under this, there is a note of how many points have been earned (each dollar spent is one point earned), and a certain amount of points can be converted into a free product. On the opposite side, there is a mention of how much money has been saved through deals specifically.

Below this, there are various buttons including:

  • Purchases: details items purchased and total spent on different days if Safeway Membership was obtained at checkout.

  • Pharmacy & Health: Lists information for those using Safeway as a provider for pharmaceutical products.

  • FreshPass: Information to Safeway's delivery subscription

  • Schedule and Save: Option to preplan repeated purchases to save 5% more.

  • Wallet: Information about deals and rewards, barcode for Safeway Membership, saved payment methods, and past receipts.
