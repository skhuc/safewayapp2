Clipping Coupons while In-Store

Though pre-planning is helpful, deals can sometimes come to your attention whilst in the store. It is possible to use the Safeway App and attach deals to your account physically.

  1. Locate your desired item.
    1. Look for the standard yellow "Member Price" tag.
    2. Ensure there is a white box with a barcode, labelled "digital coupon" included on the tag.
  2. Open the Safeway App on your phone.
  3. On the Home page, locate the barcode scanner, represented by the barcode symbol at the right end of the search bar.
  4. Once the camera opens up, capture the barcode for the digital coupon in the allotted box.
  5. Click "Clip deal" and view the coupon in your account.
The coupon will enact when presenting proof of membership at checkout.
