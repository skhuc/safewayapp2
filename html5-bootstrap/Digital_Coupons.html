Digital Coupons

Items with digital coupons have a specially reduced price.

The Deals+ page lists all the products with a coupon attached to them. On the app, there is a "Clip deal" button under the listing that will attach the deal to the member's account. In person, these items will have a barcode labeled "digital coupon" alongside the price tag that members can scan. For the coupon price to apply, members must have the specific product's coupon attached to their account upon check-out. The decreased coupon price may also be a "Buy One Get One," or "BOGO" deal, where buying one item may lead to the second having a decreased or no cost.

These items are usually priced down further than the member deals attached to the Weekly Ad, or as presented in-person on the yellow "Member Deal" price tag. These items have an expiry date, but the coupon deal is usually valid past the week time period of the Weekly Ad deals. However, the expiration dates can vary.
