List of other App Features

There are various features on the Safeway App that are unrelated to finding deals/groceries in general that users may be interested in getting familiar with.

Health

Various sections of the Safeway App are targeted towards users who visit Safeway for prescription fills, or would like to use the app to learn nutrition. On the Home and Member pages, users can access the "Health" view of the app to view these features. A main feature of the Health view is the pharmacy access, where users can fill and refill prescriptions, transfer Rx's, etc.

Meal Plans

On the Home page, there is a button labelled "Meal Plans," which gives users access to recipes depending on their dietary restrictions and habits.

FreshPass

FreshPass is a paid subscription service for grocery delivery.

Play & Earn/Points System

When linking a membership to in-store purchases, members can earn "points" for the dollar amount they spend, which can accumulate to earn free items. For certain items/times, there may be more points earned for dollar amount spent, which can be found in the "Play & Earn" section.

Store Mode

Users can view the app in "Store Mode," found on the home page, which adjusts the view.
